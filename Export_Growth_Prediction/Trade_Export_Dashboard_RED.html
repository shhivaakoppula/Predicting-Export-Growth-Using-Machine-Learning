Tariff Structure & Trade Policy
MFN liberalisation trend, AHS burden, and duty-free access impact
Average MFN Tariff Rate 1988–2021 — WTO Liberalisation Trend
How have Most Favoured Nation tariff rates evolved across 34 years of WTO negotiations?
Impact: Tariffs fell from ~15% (1990) to <9% (2021). This structural decline partly explains rising export growth probabilities — and why MFN_Simple_Average is the top ML feature.
AHS Tariff Burden vs Export Growth Probability
Do countries with lower applied tariffs have higher growth probability?
Impact: Negative correlation confirmed — validates WTO liberalisation policy and AHS_Simple_Average as a key predictor.
Duty-Free Share Impact on Export Growth
Do countries with greater duty-free market access export more?
Impact: Q4 (high duty-free access) economies grow exports at 2× the rate of Q1 economies.

Avg YoY Export Growth by Decade
Which decades were the strongest for global export expansion?

Export Growth: Surplus vs Deficit Economies
Do trade surplus countries grow exports faster?
Impact: Surplus economies grow at ~6.8% vs ~3.2% for deficit — validates Surplus_Flag as a binary ML feature.
